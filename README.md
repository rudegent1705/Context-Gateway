## 📖 What is Context-Gateway?

Context-Gateway is a tool designed to improve how AI agents handle their memory and tasks. It acts like a smart helper that keeps track of everything an AI agent does. This helps the AI work faster and smarter by cleaning up old information and organizing current context without losing important details.

You don’t need any special knowledge to use Context-Gateway. It works behind the scenes to make your AI tools more efficient. This means your AI can focus on the job with less distraction from irrelevant data.

## 💻 System Requirements

To use Context-Gateway, make sure your computer meets these basic needs:

- **Operating System:** Windows 10 or later, macOS 10.14 or later, or a modern Linux distribution.
- **Processor:** Any 64-bit CPU with at least 2 cores.
- **Memory (RAM):** Minimum 4GB. More is better for larger AI tasks.
- **Storage:** At least 500MB of free space.
- **Internet:** Required for downloading and some AI agent connections.

Your computer likely already meets these. If not, upgrading your system will help get the best experience.

## 🚀 How to Use Context-Gateway

After installing, launch Context-Gateway from your desktop or applications menu.

**Step 1: Connect Your AI Agent**

Context-Gateway works as a middleman between you and your AI agent. You will need to connect it to your AI system by entering your AI service details in the app. This may include keys or URLs if you use online AI tools.

**Step 2: Start the Proxy**

Click the “Start” button inside Context-Gateway. It will begin monitoring your AI agent’s communication.

**Step 3: Manage Context**

The app will automatically keep your AI's memory clean. It saves your AI from handling too much old information by compacting history and optimizing what your AI sees.

**Step 4: Adjust Settings**

Explore options to change how much history Context-Gateway remembers. You can choose shorter or longer memory depending on your task needs.

**Step 5: Stop When Done**

When finished, click “Stop” to end proxying.

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

If you run into problems, try these tips:

- **Download Issues:** Make sure your internet connection is active and large files can download.
- **Install Errors:** Close other programs, then run the installer again as administrator (right-click and choose “Run as administrator” on Windows).
- **AI Connection Problems:** Check your AI keys or URLs are correct. Your AI provider should give you these details.
- **Proxy Doesn’t Start:** Restart Context-Gateway or your computer.
- **App Freezes or Crashes:** Update to the latest version from the release page. If the problem continues, note any error messages.
